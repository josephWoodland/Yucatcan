ABOUT US

Yucatan Ldn launched in March 2018 as a street food company specializing is the cuisine of Southern Mexico with an LA twist to it in terms of presenting it. Utilizing recipes learned while working in the Food Service industry in California, the most important component of our food is the freshness of it. Why we stand out from other Mexican restaurants is because we specialize in Southern Mexican Cuisine with an LA twist.

The tortillas & the salsas are the most important components of Mexican food. Southern Mexican cuisine differs tremendously than the rest of Mexico due to its geographical location & climate. Most of Mexico is deserts with cactuses but in the south it’s a tropical ecosystem so what grows there and what they eat is entirely different than in rest of Mexico.

The flavors I associate with Yucatan are savory, spicy, sweet, and fresh. Southern Mexican food is very light, very healthy, and very fresh. Its fast to serve and has every bit of balance in flavor any foodie loves. The experience you will get at Yucatan Ldn will be very fun, chilled out, and you wanting more food. Since we have been in business we have been all over London, serving thousands of meals per year in places like Shoreditch, Notting Hill, Camden Town, really all over London.

founder

Spencer Wellman is a native of Los Angeles. Having lived in San Francisco for many years he has worked at some of the best restaurants in the world including The Slanted Door, The French Laundry, OTD Bush, and also The Ledbury in London. While working in the restaurants in San Francisco he picked up on some amazing southern Mexican dishes the chefs would make for the staff to eat on there breaks.

Most of the chefs hailed from Yucatan in the south. When he moved to London he knew Mexican food was seriously missing out here. So he would host Taco Tuesday nights at his house with all of his friends and that turned into taking it the next step and launching at the local street market near his house.

Since then he has done over 30-40 venues including pub, warehouses, big & small venues for private events and markets. He still to this day doesn’t feel like this his job, simply put he loves every part of it. Spencer has been a whipping up these tacos for 3 years now in London.
